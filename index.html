Bitcoin Simulation Data Deletion

At Bitcoin Simulation, we prioritize the privacy and security of your personal information. If you wish to have your data deleted from our records, you've come to the right place. This page will guide you through the process of submitting a data deletion request.

Steps for Data Deletion:

Data deletion can be performed by verifyingyour identity through Google login.

1. To ensure the security of your data, we need to verify your identity. Please verify yourself by clicking Login with Google button.

2. After successful verification, you will see Delete Bitcoin Simulation button.

3. Click on Delete Bitcoin Simulation button and give confirmation to delete data.

It will immediately delete all your data from Bitcoin Simulation except user's device information such as device id, ad id, device name, device version. We require this information for security and to detect fraud, abuse and illegal activity on the Platform.

Important Information:

Irreversible Process:

Data deletion is an irreversible process. Once your data is deleted, it cannot be recovered. Please make sure you want to proceed before data deletion.
